About Company

Golden Diamond Engineering Pty Ltd(GDE) is a new, level 1 BBBEE, engineering and project management consultancy based in Southern Africa. GDE was established in the year 2020.

Mission

We provide engineering consultancy and project management services within RSA to public and private sector clients.

Vission

We shall be your business partner of choice for your engineering and project management consulting needs in South Africa.

Our Key Strength

GDE values and beliefs

Our values and beliefs constitute a set of principles that ensure consistency between our actions and our promises. To GDE, doing business in the right manner and delivering the right results is a way of life.

Adaptability

we are cognizant of the ever changing needs of our clients and business environment. We therefore remain open to make fast changes to suit new realities as we seek to deliver our promise.

Synergy/Leverage

We recognize the need to leverage on the best that others have to offer to complement and supplement our value proposition. We have a pool of able and willing partners to supplement our offering with diverse skills.
